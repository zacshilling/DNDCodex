Sword Coast North

General Information

This region stretches from just south of the spine of the world, down to the city of Waterdeep, is bordered by the Sea of Moving Ice in the west and the Long Road in the east.

The main cities of the Sword Coast North are:

  • Mirabar
  • Luskan
  • Neverwinter
  • Longsaddle
  • Triboar

The main geographical features of the region are:

  • The Crags
  • Neverwinter Wood
  • The Sword Mountains
  • The Mere of Dead Ment
  • The River Mirar

The minor settlements and villages of this region are:

  • Phandalin

Other Significant Sites on the Sword Coast North are:

  • Castle Heckler
